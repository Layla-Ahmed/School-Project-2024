Game Highlights

Welcome to the wild world of Professor Hamilton Obby, where every step is a test of your skills and courage! Dive into mind-bending levels, filled with gravity-defying platforms and towering obstacles that only the bravest will beat. Each level is an adventure of its own! But watch out! One wrong move, and it's back to square one. Can you outsmart the traps and emerge victorious?

How to Play the Game - Quick Start Guide

  • Step 1: Log in, sign up or play without an account! To customise your character, it is compulsory to make an account.

  • Step 2: Learn the controls and familiarize yourself with the basics. Use WASD and the space bar to move on a computer, and the joystick on mobile.

  • Step 3: Try to beat the first few easier levels. Be careful, they get progressively harder!

  • Step 4: Try to make your way through all 30+ levels.

  • Step 5: Challenge your friends and try to secure a spot on the leaderboard!

Controls for Desktop and Mobile

Desktop:

  • WASD Keys: Move
  • Space bar: Jump
  • Shift: Walk Slowly
  • C Key: Crouch
  • C Key while running: Slide
  • Left mouse button: Hold and drag to look around

Mobile:

  • Joystick: Move
  • Jump Button: Tap to Jump
  • Crouch Button: Tap to Crouch
  • Crouch Button while running: Slide
  • Drag Screen: Look around

Falling Off

Watch your step! Falling could result in restarting from the last checkpoint.

Checkpoints

Checkpoints are at the end of every level, so you won't lose all your progress if you make a mistake.
