Quick Reads, Big Adventures

Welcome to our world of short stories, where quick reads turn into wild adventures, unexpected twists, and unforgettable laughs! Here, each story is a bite-sized journey into the daily mishaps and mysteries of our characters. Whether it’s Professor Harry Hamilton’s latest invention going hilariously wrong or his quirky neighbors getting tangled up in bizarre situations, these tales are packed with humor, heart, and a hint of the unexpected. Perfect for a quick escape, our short stories are crafted to bring a smile, surprise, or even a chuckle out loud. So, dive in to discover what’s new in Hamilton’s world. These stories are as unpredictable as they are fun—you never know what will happen next!

Behind the Story

Our journey began a few years ago when I sketched a cat wearing glasses in my notebook. My best friend, Thimna, named him 'Professor Harry Hamilton,' inspired by our Natural Science teacher, Mr. Hastie, who also wore glasses and conducted experiments. But the real spark for our stories came when we saw Mr. Ronander, a school worker, riding his lawnmower. We instantly thought, 'What if we gave our quirky character a story of his own?' With that idea—and a touch of imagination—Professor Hamilton's lawnmower became a Pancake Mower, adding the funny twist we wanted. The first characters to join were Harry, along with his teenage neighbors Fred and Tom. Since then, we’ve added more books and comics to our collection, eventually inspiring a website dedicated to our wacky world. Over time, we've expanded and refined the site to become what you see today: a place for all things hilarious and Hamilton!
